Meta Bulk Ad Uploader

Upload a spreadsheet of ads. Everything is created as PAUSED — review in Ads Manager before activating.

1 Download the template

Excel file with dropdowns for objective, optimization goal, CTA, etc. Fill in one row per ad, then come back.

⬇ Download template.xlsx

2 Provide your filled spreadsheet

Paste your Google Sheets URL. The sheet must be shared with Anyone with the link → Viewer (Share button top-right of the sheet). The gid in the URL picks the specific tab.
— or —
Accepts .xlsx or .csv. Rows sharing a campaign_name become one campaign; same campaign_name + adset_name become one ad set.

3 Meta credentials

Generate at Graph API Explorer with ads_management scope. Saved locally in your browser so you don't have to paste again.
Top-left account dropdown in Ads Manager. Include the act_ prefix.
